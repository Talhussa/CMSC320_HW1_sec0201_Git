Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines 2 branches by making a new merge commit that preserves the true history. Rebasing rewrites your branch's commits on top of another branch to produce a cleaner, more linear history, at the cost of altering commit hashes.
